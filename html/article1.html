Marvel Unlimited Ends the Year with Major Infinity Comics Moments

BY MEAGAN DAMORE

Only one month remains in the year, but Marvel Unlimited has no plans to slow down. In fact, subscribers should prepare themselves for a heart-wrenching, action-packed couple of weeks across Infinity Comics titles like IT’S JEFF, AVENGERS ACADEMY: MARVEL’S VOICES, and ASTONISHING X-MEN!

Starting now and continuing through next Wednesday, December 4, Marvel Unlimited will conclude X-MEN: FROM THE ASHES before launching the all-new ASTONISHING X-MEN title, kick off another season of IT’S JEFF with Jeff the Land Shark, bring the Scarlet Witch’s sons Billy and Tommy to AVENGERS ACADEMY: MARVEL’S VOICES, and so much more. Check out the full list of Infinity Comics milestones to come for more details!

X-MEN: FROM THE ASHES SERIES FINALE

It’s Thanksgiving Day and Beak is feeling defeated. Will a chance encounter in the airport grant the family a holiday miracle?

ASTONISHING SPIDER-MAN FIRST ARC FINALE

Alive all this time? Crime Lord’s shocking confession has us all on the edge of our seats. Is he really who he says he is? Spider-Man’s dying to find out.

IT’S JEFF SEASON PREMIERE

A brand-new season of the beloved IT’S JEFF! It’s time, True Believers, for the little land shark to pull his own weight. Jeff is off to work! Will he make his quarterly goals?!?!

MARVEL MEOW SEASON FINALE

Chewie’s feelings are hurt after Captain Marvel left her behind on a mission. Can Carol ever make it up to her?

ASTONISHING X-MEN SERIES PREMIERE

Husk returns to her childhood home in Kentucky as her old professor, Banshee, arrives in New York looking for his daughter. They don’t realize that both of their families are caught up in something bigger than they know!

AVENGERS ACADEMY: MARVEL’S VOICES NEW ARC

“The Angel of History” – Secrets of the lady M’Kraan are finally revealed as Mephisto narrows his sights on Avengers Academy!

HOUSE OF HARKNESS ARC FINALE

After a stunning revelation about the House of Harkness, Wanda is forced to choose a side…
